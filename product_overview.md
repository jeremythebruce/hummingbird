# Product Overview

Hummingbird is a platform designed to improve the real estate transaction experience for buyers and sellers.

## Key Concepts

- Simplified transaction workflows
- Reduced reliance on intermediaries
- Integrated service ecosystem
- Transparent process visibility
- User-centric design

## Direction

The product is being refined through structured analysis of existing market limitations and user experience gaps.

## Notes

Detailed product mechanics and workflows are maintained internally.
