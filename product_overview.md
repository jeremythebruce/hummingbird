# Product Overview

Hummingbird is a platform designed to enable buyers and sellers to transact real estate without relying on a traditional agent or broker.

## Core Principles

- Direct interaction between buyers and sellers
- Simplified transaction workflows
- Transparent and data-driven decision making
- Integrated services such as lenders and title companies
- Trust and identity-first design

## Problem

Traditional real estate transactions are complex, costly, and dependent on intermediaries that do not always provide proportional value.

## Solution

Hummingbird replaces the traditional model with a streamlined, technology-driven platform that empowers users to manage transactions directly while still accessing necessary services.
